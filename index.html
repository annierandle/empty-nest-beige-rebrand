MY STORY

Meet Kellie

THE JOURNEY SO FAR

From helping my kids navigate their first apartments to finally taking those dream trips with my husband of 32 years, this stage is filled with unexpected joys and fresh possibilities.

Recently, I shared our biggest transition yet: moving our family across the country from California to Tennessee. The entire journey of selling our family home, starting fresh in a new community, and making friends in our 50s has been equal parts terrifying and exhilarating.

WHAT LIGHTS ME UP

When I'm not planning family gatherings or helping with apartment decorating, you'll find me grocery shopping or cooking, playing pickleball, learning golf, or running our award-winning PR company. My tips have been featured on TV, podcasts, and magazines, but my real passion is inspiring parents to embrace and enjoy this incredible new chapter.
